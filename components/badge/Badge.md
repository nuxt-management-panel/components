## Badge

### sample
```html
<Badge/>
```

### props

> name:String

> dismiss:Boolean
> 
> *show or hide close button*

> badge:String
> - circle
> - rounded
>
> *it defines type of badge*

> size:String
>  - lg
>  - sm

> design:String
>  - text-danger , text-danger , ...
>  - bg-danger , bg-light-danger , ....

> showIcon:Boolean

> icon:String
> 
> *sends font awesome classes*

### emit
> onDismiss
> - *write some codes to close badge and send some request to server*
